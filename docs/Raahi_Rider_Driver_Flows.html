1. Rider Flow — Complete Diagram

flowchart TD
    START([App Launch]) --> SPLASH[Splash]
    SPLASH --> HEALTH{Backend OK?}
    HEALTH -->|No| ERR[Connection Error]
    HEALTH -->|Yes| INIT{First setup?}
    INIT -->|No server| SRV["/settings/server"]
    INIT -->|No welcome| WEL["/welcome-onboarding"]
    INIT -->|Ready| LOGIN["/login"]

    LOGIN --> L_OTP[Get OTP inline]
    LOGIN --> L_GOO[Google Sign-In]
    LOGIN --> L_TC[Truecaller]
    L_OTP -->|New user| NAME["/name entry"]
    L_OTP -->|Returning| HOME
    L_GOO --> PHONE{Needs phone?}
    L_TC --> PHONE
    PHONE -->|Yes| PLINK["/phone-number link"]
    PHONE -->|New| NAME
    PHONE -->|Existing| HOME
    NAME --> HOME

    HOME["/ Home gateway"]
    HOME -->|Find a Ride| SVC
    HOME -->|Banner searching| SRCH
    HOME -->|Banner in-ride| LIVE
    HOME -->|Switch Account| SHEET1[[Switch sheet]]
    SHEET1 -->|Logout| LOGIN

    SVC["/services hub"]
    SVC -->|Profile| PROF
    SVC -->|Service/search| FT
    SVC -->|Schedule Later| FT
    SVC -->|Active ride| LIVE
    SVC -->|Pending search| SRCH

    FT["/booking/find-trip"]

    FT -->|Drawer Rides| HIST
    FT -->|Drawer Pay/Help| FT
    FT -->|Drawer Profile| PROF
    FT -->|Back| SVC

    PAY["/booking/payment"]
    PAY -->|Slide confirm| SRCH
    PAY -->|Back| FT

    SRCH["/booking/searching"]
    SRCH -->|Driver found| LIVE
    SRCH -->|Cancel| FT
    SRCH -->|Minimize| SVC

    LIVE["/booking/driver-assigned"]
    LIVE -->|Chat| CHAT[[Chat sheet]]
    LIVE -->|Complete| RATE[[Rating sheet]]
    RATE --> HOME
    LIVE -->|Cancel| HOME
    LIVE -->|Back| SVC

    PROF["/profile"]
    PROF --> HIST
    PROF -->|Saved/Settings| PROF
    PROF -->|Switch Driver| HOME
    PROF -->|Logout| LOGIN

    PUSH --> SVC
    PUSH --> HIST

flowchart TD
