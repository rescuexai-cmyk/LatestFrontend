# Raahi Pricing: Simple, Transparent, Fair

## What you pay for

Your trip fare is built from clear parts:

- Base fare (starting charge)
- Distance fare (per km)
- Time fare (per minute)
- Extra charges only when applicable (tolls, parking, waiting, airport fee, extra stops)
- Taxes
- Discounts/promos (if applied)

No hidden line items. You can see a full fare breakdown in-app.

## How surge pricing works

Sometimes demand is high and driver availability is low. In those moments, dynamic pricing (surge) may apply.

- Surge is shown as a multiplier (example: `1.3x`)
- You see the effect before booking
- When surge ends, normal pricing returns automatically

## Why your fare can differ trip-to-trip

Fare can change based on:

- Route distance and travel time
- Traffic conditions
- Time of day / peak demand
- Tolls, airport zones, waiting time, extra stops
- Vehicle category selected

## Vehicle options and pricing

Raahi supports multiple categories (for example: Bike Rescue, Auto, Cab Mini, Cab XL, Premium).  
Each category has its own fare range so you can choose speed, comfort, and budget.

## What makes Raahi pricing trustworthy

- Upfront fare visibility before confirming
- Transparent breakdown, not a black box
- Clear surge indication when active
- Category-based options for better control
- Fallback pricing protection to keep booking available during temporary backend issues

## Quick FAQ

**Q: Is surge always active?**  
No. Surge is only active during high-demand windows.

**Q: Will I know surge before booking?**  
Yes. Surge is shown in the app before you confirm.

**Q: Do I get a receipt with details?**  
Yes. Ride details include fare components and final total.
